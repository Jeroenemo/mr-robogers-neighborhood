Welcome to the Mr. Rogers Factory!

I know this is your first day, so you must be a little confused. Your job is to run some tests on the Mr. Rogerbots as they come down the assembly line. They will ask you for your name, and a whole number. Last name is optional, of course. They shoud able to count from 0 to your number, and in reverse! Fair warning, any number that contains one, two, or three might make them buzz with glee!
